    üí° Study Tips & Tricks

    Tip 1: Active Recall

    Test yourself on material without looking at your notes. This strengthens memory.

    Tip 2: Spaced Repetition

    Review material over increasing intervals (1 day, 3 days, 1 week) to build long-term memory.

    Tip 3: Feynman Technique

    Explain concepts in simple terms. If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it well enough.

    Tip 4: Environment Matters

    Study in a quiet, well-lit space. Remove distractions like phones to improve focus.

    Tip 5: Mind Mapping

    Create visual diagrams connecting related concepts. This helps organize and remember information.
